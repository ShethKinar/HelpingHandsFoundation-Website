About us

The Helping Hands Foundation is a non-profit organization, which works to help financially weaker student for education. Helping Hands foundation Rajkot was founded 5 years ago by few friends with a moral of giving back to society and being responsible citizen . Then many more kind hearted people joined them and eventually it became a chain. At present Helping Hands Foundation Rajkot is a group of more than 200 members , which shows there are many people in our society who believes that helping others is the best act of a human.

Motive

There are many children in the country who are deprived of education. Education is everyone’s fundamental right that is why no child of any section of the society should be deprived of education just because they are poor. The motive is very clear , To help students of weaker financial condition or in trouble due to circumstances and provide them equal opportunity of education. And to build a society with well educated, well mannered and nice citizens in the country, Which can help to reform the nation in better way.
